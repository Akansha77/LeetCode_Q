### D8Q2: All leaf nodes at same level

Given a Binary Tree, check if all leaves are at same level or not.

Example 1:
```
Input: 
            1
          /   \
         2     3
```
Output: 1

Explanation:  Leaves 2 and 3 are at same level.

Example 2:
```
Input:
            10
          /    \
        20      30
       /  \        
     10    15
```
Output: 0

Explanation:  Leaves 10, 15 and 30 are not at same level.
